Waps/happening SxP Films

Record breaker — we finally adapt the playful tone on "can we just chats?"

With "can we just chats?", we explored a more playful and energetic approach to storytelling. Rather than opening with a serious narrative, we focused on the genuine chemistry between three teenagers during a sleepover, capturing the laughter, spontaneity, and carefree moments that make friendships feel real and relatable. To strengthen this atmosphere, we embraced dynamic camera movements, vibrant lighting, and natural performances that allowed every interaction to unfold effortlessly. This playful tone serves a greater purpose than entertainment alone — by immersing audiences in the characters' joyful moments, the film creates a meaningful contrast as the story gradually shifts toward its central message about how social media can quietly distance people, even when they're sharing the same space.
